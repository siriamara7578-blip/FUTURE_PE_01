We follow a simple system:

**1. Teach Clearly**

Students solve problems every class — not just listen.

**3. Weekly Tests**
Every week tests are conducted. This removes exam fear and builds writing speed.

**4. Doubt Clearing Sessions**
No doubt is ignored. Students can ask questions freely until fully clear.

**5. Exam Preparation Strategy**
We teach:

* which questions to attempt first
* how to present answers
* how to score step marks

This is where many students gain **10–25 extra marks**.
